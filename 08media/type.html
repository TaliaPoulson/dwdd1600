Which Stick Type is Best For You?

Standard Drumsticks

5A
All-purpose stick, good for beginners and intermediate players.
5B
Slightly thicker than 5A, suitable for rock and heavier playing styles.
7A
Lighter and thinner than 5A, ideal for jazz and lighter playing styles.

Heavy and Performance Sticks

2B
Thicker and heavier than 5B, great for powerful playing and louder music genres.
3A
A balance between 5A and 2B, versatile for various music styles.

Specialty and Extended Length Sticks

  • 8D: Longer and thinner, often used for marching bands and orchestral settings.
  • 1A: Heavier than 2B, suitable for heavy rock and metal genres.
  • 1B: Similar to 1A but slightly lighter, good for rock and hard-hitting styles.
